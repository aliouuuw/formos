# Formos

register: product

## Product Purpose
Internal lead-generation form platform for Everest Finance. Staff build multi-step forms (Typeform-style), publish them to `/f/slug`, and track submissions, leads, and funnel analytics.

## Users
Everest Finance marketing and sales staff. Non-technical builders creating mortgage/finance inquiry flows; sales reps reviewing leads and submissions. Used at a desk, daytime, on laptops and large monitors.

## Brand & Tone
Calm, premium, financial-grade trust. Quiet confidence, not startup-loud. Copy is short and directive.

## Visual Language
- Light theme. Warm paper background (`bone`), ink text (`night`), deep plum accent (`mauve`), forest `everest-green` for positive/live states, `gold` reserved for rare highlights.
- Rounded-xl geometry, hairline `mauve/10` borders, soft single-layer elevation.
- One sans family. Labels in 11px uppercase tracking-wide mauve-60.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS dashboards (hero metric rows, identical card grids).
- Dark "tool" aesthetics, neon, glassmorphism.
- Typeform's UI itself: match its editing *experience* (live preview, structure rail), not its look.

## Strategic Principles
- The builder is the product. Editing should feel live: every change visible immediately.
- Building and reviewing results are separate modes; never mix them on one surface.
- Familiar affordances over invention. The tool disappears into the task.
